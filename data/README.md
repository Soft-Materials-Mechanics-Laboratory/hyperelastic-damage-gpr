# Data

This folder contains all datasets used in this study.
Note that experimental data of brain tissue tensile tests were digitized from Franceschini et al. (G. Franceschini, D. Bigoni, P. Regitnig, G. A. Holzapfel, Brain tissue deforms similarly to filled elastomers and follows consolidation theory, Journal of the Mechanics and Physics of Solids 54 (12) (2006) 2592–2620. doi:10.1016/j.jmps.2006.05.004.)
